# Data Availability Statement

The datasets, experimental configurations, notebooks, and generated results supporting this study are publicly available in this repository.

## Original Dataset

The real-world dataset used in this study is the Electricity Dataset, which is publicly available through OpenML:

Dataset Name: Electricity

OpenML Dataset ID: 151

Source:
[https://www.openml.org/d/151](https://www.openml.org/search?type=data&sort=runs&id=151&status=active)

## Synthetic Datasets

Two synthetic datasets generated for this research are included in the repository:

* Synthetic Gradual Drift Dataset
* Synthetic Abrupt Drift Dataset

These datasets were derived from the original Electricity dataset to simulate controlled concept drift scenarios for experimental evaluation.

## Experimental Artifacts

The repository also includes:

* Data preprocessing notebooks
* Synthetic data generation notebooks
* Experiment execution notebooks
* Hyperparameter configurations
* Figures and tables reported in the paper

These materials are provided to support transparency, reproducibility, and future research on selective retraining under concept drift.
